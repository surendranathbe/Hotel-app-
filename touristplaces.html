Statue of Equality

A massive statue of Ramanuja, recently inaugurated near Shamshabad. The statue stands 216 feet tall and promotes the values of equality and spirituality.

Tummalapalli Kalakshetram (Modern Cultural Center)

A modern auditorium and cultural center showcasing music, dance, and heritage events. Attracts both tourists and locals for cultural experiences.

Durgam Cheruvu Cable Bridge

Hyderabad's newest iconic bridge offering scenic views, cycling paths, walking trails, and a modern urban vibe. Great for evening visits and photos.

Snow World (Updated Attractions)

Though not brand new, Snow World has upgraded its snow activities and virtual reality games. A perfect escape for families looking for indoor fun.
